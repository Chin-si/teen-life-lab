# Teen Life Lab – Addictive Quizzes 🌟

**Discover your personality, social style, and unlock secret quizzes!**  
A fun, interactive, and addictive frontend-only web app designed for teens to explore love, friendship, social life, and enjoy daily challenges — all in one place.

---

## Features

- **Main Personality & Social Quiz**  
  Predict your social energy, personality traits, and teen lifestyle.  

- **Secret Unlockable Quizzes**  
  Randomly unlock secret quizzes for TikTok trends, love compatibility, friendship superpowers, social media persona, and more.  

- **Random Rewards & Bonuses**  
  Complete quizzes and challenges to get random streak bonuses, unlock secret quizzes, and collect fun insights.  

- **Daily Challenges**  
  Encourages users to return every day with new challenges. Includes streak tracking and rare bonus events.  

- **Progress Tracker**  
  Monitor your completion status of all secret quizzes with a fun progress display.  

- **Shareable Results**  
  Easily share your quiz results on social media or copy a link for friends.  

- **Downloadable Challenge Images**  
  Turn daily challenges into a fun image to save or share.  

- **Frontend-Only & Fully Responsive**  
  Works perfectly on **desktop, tablet, and mobile**. No backend required.  

- **Google AdSense Ready**  
  Includes a placeholder for monetization with Google AdSense.

---

## How to Use

1. **Start the Quiz** – Press the "Start Quiz" button to begin the main quiz.  
2. **Answer Questions** – Choose the option that fits you best.  
3. **Get Results & Rewards** – Your result will feel personal and fun. You may unlock secret quizzes randomly!  
4. **Check Secret Quizzes Progress** – Track which quizzes you’ve completed.  
5. **Complete Daily Challenges** – Return daily to complete challenges and maintain streaks.  
6. **Share & Download** – Share results with friends or download your daily challenge as an image.  

---

## Deployment

This app is **frontend-only** and can be deployed on **GitHub Pages**:

1. Save the main code as `index.html`.  
2. Create a GitHub repository and upload the file.  
3. Go to **Settings → Pages → Source → main branch → /root**.  
4. Your app will be live at `https://USERNAME.github.io/REPO_NAME/`.  

---

## Customization

- Replace the **AdSense placeholder** with your AdSense script to monetize.  
- Add new quizzes or questions in the `mainQuestions` or `secretQuizzes` arrays in the HTML file.  
- Update daily challenges in the `challenges` array.
